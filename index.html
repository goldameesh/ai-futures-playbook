‚ùì FAQ

How do I verify if a course is legitimate?

For UAE: Check KHDA's official website (khda.gov.ae)

For India: Look for ISO certification, verified reviews

For all: Read recent reviews, verify credentials, check refund policies

Are the "free" courses really free?

Code.org, MIT Day of AI, Hour of AI, AI4K12, Elements of AI are completely free.

Google AI Essentials typically requires Coursera subscription (~$49/month) but may be free via some schools/employers.

How much should I expect to spend?

You can complete the entire 3-year journey for FREE using the free courses listed.

If you choose paid options:

  • Budget: $40-80/month
  • Mid-range: $200-400 for structured programs
  • Premium: $900-1,850+ for intensive programs
