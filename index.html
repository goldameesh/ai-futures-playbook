About This Playbook

Foreword by Amish Shah

In a world where artificial intelligence is reshaping every facet of life, preparing our children isn't just an option; it's essential.

As an educational futurist, I've dedicated my career to bridging technology and human potential. This playbook draws from global best practices, including the World Economic Forum's insights and pioneering curricula in the UAE and India.

Why this playbook? Because AI evolves at breakneck speed, but the real power lies in humans who can guide it responsibly.

3-Year Learning Journey

1

Sparking Curiosity

Ages 8-10 | Year 1: December 2025 - November 2026

Focus: Unplugged activities, pattern recognition, basic ethics

Weekly Time: 2 hours

Core Activities

‚ñº
  • Pattern Detectives: Sort toys/objects to understand how AI learns from data
  • Ethics Stories: Role-play scenarios about fair AI
  • Real-World Hunt: Find patterns in weather, family routines, nature
  • AI Adventure Journal: Weekly reflections on discoveries

Recommended Courses

‚ñº

Free Options:

  • Code.org - AI for Oceans (6-8 hours)
  • Hour of AI by CSforALL (1 hour)
  • Google AI Explorers (4-6 hours)

Milestones

‚ñº
  • Month 3: Created "Fair Robot Friend" rules poster
  • Month 6: Completed one free course
  • Month 9: Built a pattern prediction game
  • Month 12: Filled 50+ journal entries
2

Hands-On Exploration

Ages 11-12 | Year 2: December 2026 - November 2027

Focus: Visual coding, bias detection, interactive projects

Weekly Time: 2.5 hours

Core Projects

‚ñº
  • Scratch Game Bot: Create a game where AI learns from player behavior
  • Bias Detective Challenge: Test real systems for fairness
  • Fair System Design: Build a recommendation system with fairness rules
3

Adaptive Innovation

Ages 13-14 | Year 3: December 2027 - November 2028

Focus: Real-world solutions, capstone projects, community impact

Weekly Time: 3 hours

Capstone Project Framework

‚ñº

Months 1-3: Discover & Define - Community needs assessment, problem selection, ethics review

Months 4-8: Build & Test - Prototype development, user testing, refinement

Months 9-12: Launch & Reflect - Community showcase, impact measurement, portfolio building
